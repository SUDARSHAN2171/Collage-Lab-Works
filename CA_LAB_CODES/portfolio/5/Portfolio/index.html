Who I am

Junior JavaScript developer based out of Oslo

In 2022 I decided to make a career change, going from the science field to becoming a JavaScript developer. I was accepted into a 3 month intensive program that was held by Academic Work Academy and Brights Learning. I completed the program alongside 17 other candidates. It was one of the fastest and greatest learning experiences I've had.

In the span of only 12 weeks, I learned the basics of Fullstack JavaScript - how to incorporate front-end and back-end solutions with a database to make a full functioning app. Some of the topics covered included, but not limited to were UX/UI design, HTML, CSS and React, Node.js with Express JS, APIs and how to connect the server to a postgreSQL database. Additionally, we touched on how to work in an Agile team. I'm looking forward to where my new career takes me.
